IMPENDING TRIP TO THE ZOO

The children are expected to go on a trip to the zoo. This is intended to enhance the children's knowledge on diffeent animal spiecies and to also instill the spirit of togetherness among them. Parents are thus requested to collaborate with the school to ensure a smooth trip fpr the children.

One good turn deserves another. Having won the previous annual African football championships, Hill Academy successfully defended their crown. They beat Seeta Junior on the final. The next annual football championship will be hosted by the School.

Excellence in the world of academia is a top priority at Hill Academy. The school therefore holds a graduation ceremony for the graduands as they advance from one level to another in the academic pyramid.
